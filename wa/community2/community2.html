ANTS LOVE NETWORKS!

Typically tunnel networks. But given the power of the internet they could do wonders... this website is a welcome spot for when they do. Keeping up with the weather is all the more important when you're the size of a raindrop- access to this information could save lives. Alerts on predators, food sightings, and colony data could all benefit ants immensely.
